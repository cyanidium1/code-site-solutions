# Frequently asked questions

## General

### What is this company?

Code Site Art is a boutique custom web development studio, founded in 2023 by Fedir Alpatov, a developer and tech lead. The studio is based in Kyiv, Ukraine, and works remotely; the English version of the site serves the UK market. Projects are built on Next.js, React, TypeScript and Sanity CMS. Clients own the deliverables: source code, hosting, domain and data. More: https://www.code-site.art/en/about.md

### What services do you provide?

Design, development and launch of custom websites and business systems — from landing pages to bespoke platforms such as a SaaS, a marketplace or a B2B portal. Every project includes SEO architecture, a Sanity CMS admin panel, integrations (forms, Telegram, CRM, payments, booking), analytics, infrastructure on the client's accounts, and post-launch support. Every project carries a 1-year warranty.

## Services

### How do I order a service?

A project starts with a free brief — a 30-minute call or a WhatsApp chat that ends with an exact price range and a plan recommendation. A contract is then signed via DocuSign or a PDF with signature; it locks the price, the deadline and a 30% rebate for delays. After a 50% deposit, design, development, testing and launch follow. Steps in detail: https://www.code-site.art/en/process. Instant estimate: https://www.code-site.art/en/calculator

### Is there a consultation?

Yes. The first stage of every project is a free brief: a 30-minute call or chat covering the task, goals, audience, budget and timeline, ending with an exact price range and a tier recommendation. Booking: https://calendly.com/fedirdev

## Terms

### How does payment work?

Payment is split in two: a 50% deposit after the contract is signed, and the final 50% at launch, after which the client receives all credentials. Prices are in GBP and fixed in the contract before work starts. Payment methods: bank transfer, card via Stripe, or Direct Debit.
