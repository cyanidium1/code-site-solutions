# About the company

## Who we are

Code Site Art is a boutique custom web development studio. It was founded in 2023 by Fedir Alpatov, a developer and tech lead who takes part in the architecture and implementation of every project; a Kyiv Polytechnic Institute graduate, building for the web since 2021. The studio is based in Kyiv, Ukraine, and works remotely. Working languages: English, Ukrainian, Russian.

Projects are built on Next.js, React, TypeScript and Sanity CMS. Clients own the deliverables: the source code lives in the client's repository, the site runs on the client's hosting accounts, and the domain and data stay under the client's control. The developer's GitHub profile is public: https://github.com/cyanidium1

## What we do

We design, build and launch custom websites and business systems — from landing pages to bespoke platforms such as a SaaS, a marketplace or a B2B portal. Every project includes:

- SEO architecture: structure, metadata and semantics built into the code from the start.
- A Sanity CMS admin panel: the client edits content and creates pages without a developer.
- Integrations: forms, Telegram, CRM, payments, booking.
- Analytics configured at launch.
- Infrastructure on the client's accounts: hosting, domain, SSL, CDN, deployment.
- Post-launch support.

Prices are in GBP and fixed in the contract before work starts. Every project carries a 1-year warranty and a 30% rebate if an agreed deadline is missed. Machine-readable pricing: https://www.code-site.art/en/pricing.md

## Who we serve

Small and medium-sized UK businesses that need a website as a working tool — leads, sales, content management. Industries served: healthcare, construction and renovation, legal, finance and accountancy, e-commerce, motor trade, property, and courses and creators. For regulated industries (healthcare, legal, accountancy, property), builds are prepared for UK GDPR / Data Protection Act 2018 compliance.
